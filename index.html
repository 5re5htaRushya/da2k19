About The Event

HEADSTART'19 the Technical Synopsium of CSE department presents you with DataAddicts. This provides the perfect platform to showcase your querying skills. It provides intriguing challenges to test your addiction for data.

SQL is an incredibly important and valuable skill employers desire. So much of our business is digital now. As organizations seek to do more with their data, they will need more individuals with the skills to access and analyze that data. SQL is the querying skill that enables you to do just that.

ROUND 1

ROUND 2

ROUND 3

Selected students from round-2 will have to execute queries on the given Database on 14th september.
